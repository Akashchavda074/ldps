### Welcome
- LPS Vidyawadi is located in the most picturesque and peaceful part of western Rajasthan. Vidyawadi is the premier educational residential institution for girls only. About 67 years ago, a few visionaries realised the importance of girls education and took initiative to make Vidyawadi a reality. With the support of the eminent donors, it continued to consolidate its sound management practices, democratic management system and infrastructural advancement.
- The sprawling campus of 65 acres houses 3 educational units: Leela Devi Parasmal Sancheti Kanya Maha vidhyalaya - B.A. / B.Com. / B.C.A (affiliated to JNV University, Jodhpur / B.A.-B.Ed Integrated Course); Marudhar Balika Vidhyapeeth (Hindi Medium) Higher Secondary School (affiliated to RBSE, Ajmer); Leela Devi Parasmal Sancheti English Medium Sr. Sec. School (affiliated to CBSE, New Delhi).
- There are 7 hostels in the campus, state of art kitchen and a dining hall, R.O drinking water plant, a huge sports ground, a gymnasium, computer labs, science labs in schools, a guest house, staff quarters and a fleet of buses to ferry students from the peripheral areas.

### About the School
- Leeladevi Parasmal Sancheti English Medium Sr. Sec. School aims at providing an excellent education to empower each student to have a true sense of self-respect which will enable her to build her identity, to find her purpose in life and to be of value to society.
- The School embraces the challenge of nurturing individual talents and strengths into valued accomplishments by providing an outstanding education that develops character, academic excellence, leadership, social responsibility and physical wellness. It is our endeavour to educate girls from classes L.K.G. to XII, with the CBSE curriculum, in a caring atmosphere.
- The management has ensured that no stone is left unturned to provide the students 'a home away from home'. The campus caters for comfortable hostels, an infirmary for minor ailments and a modern kitchen and mess providing healthy, homely and wholesome meals.
- The school offers an ethos of tradition, truth, responsibility and respect; a curriculum that meets the challenges of a global society; beautiful infrastructure and facilities for learning; special care and personal attention for every child; and an educational community committed to every child's happiness and success.

### Mission
- To provide healthy learning environment and quality education, encouraging development academically and socially. To nurture girl children, help them grow fully to their potential and become what a human being can become. The school pledges to facilitate full development of every girl child.

### Aims and Objectives
- Create and maintain a positive and encouraging psycho-social environment.
- Treat every child as equal, without prejudice.
- Provide opportunities suitable to different capabilities and encourage everyone.
- Provide learning opportunities that develop basic skills, knowledge and understanding required by modern society.
- Nurture inherent creativity in every possible domain.
- Develop critical thinking, enquiry and introspection.
- Develop self-confidence and self-esteem in every child.
- Provide high quality care and education for holistic development.
- Help young ones move from dependency to independence through self-respect, self-discipline and self-reliance.
- Promote creativity through planned co-curricular activities.
- Initiate scientific enquiry and hunger for knowledge.
- Maintain an ethos and course structure that raises every pupil's achievement.
- Inculcate love and responsibility towards fellow living beings and the nation.
- Understand and value own culture and heritage and respect other cultures.
- Promote equality and diversity, preparing children to be members of a global society.

### School Rules
- Any concern regarding a child should be given in writing to the Principal with proper student details.
- Office enquiry time on weekdays: 11:00 AM to 3:00 PM.
- Principal meeting time on weekdays: 10:00 AM to 11:00 AM only.
- Changes in address or phone number should be registered with the school office at the earliest.
- 75% attendance is compulsory in an academic year.
- Students can remain absent only with sanctioned leave.
- For emergency, the child is allowed out only with one parent or guardian.
- Reporting on the opening day is compulsory; delay costs Rs. 200 per day as fine.
- Parents are responsible for classroom learning loss during long absenteeism.
- Co-curricular activities are integral to school syllabus; participation is compulsory.
- Parents are expected to sign and follow the school diary and test papers regularly.
- Parents are requested to attend all PTMs; PTMs after half yearly and annual exams are compulsory.
- Students are not allowed to carry cell phones / I-Pod / MP3 players; if found, fine is Rs. 1000 and strict disciplinary action.
- Disciplinary action will be taken for damaging school property.
- Participation in school events is compulsory; parent support is mandatory.
- School rules may be amended; parents/students should keep track and adhere to them.

## Academic Curriculum
Source URL: https://www.lpsvidhyawadi.com/AcademicCurriculum.aspx
Capture status: Captured from live page.

### Overview
- LPS Vidyawadi offers academic curriculum from Pre-primary to Class XII. The school is affiliated to CBSE for classes L.K.G. to XII. Medium of instruction is English and curricula follow CBSE Board.

### Levels
- Pre-Primary: LKG and UKG, child-friendly classrooms, creativity through art and craft, Hindi/English letters and words, basic arithmetic.
- Primary (Std. I to V): Formal academics with focus on cognitive, emotional, social, physical and motor development; 'Learning by Doing' approach.
- Middle: Equal emphasis on curricular and co-curricular activities, CBSE guidelines, socially useful productive work and Physical and Health Education.
- Secondary: Classes IX and X focus on academics and board exam preparation, with art, craft and music for all-round development.
- Senior Secondary: Classes XI and XII provide academic and competitive knowledge, board exam preparation, and stream choice according to interest.

### Learning Support
- The school identifies each child's potential and provides support for gifted students and those with special needs.
- Parallel remedial classes support students in Mathematics, English and Hindi and help overcome academic difficulties.
- Assessment follows CBSE's competency-based pattern under the National Education Policy with periodic assessment, internal assessment, project/practical work and theory exams.
